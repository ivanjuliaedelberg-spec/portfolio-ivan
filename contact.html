Iván Juliá is an Argentinian cinematographer based between Buenos Aires and Barcelona. He graduated in Cinematography from Universidad del Cine (FUC) and later completed his studies with a Master's degree in Cinematography at ESCAC (Escola Superior de Cinema i Audiovisuals de Catalunya).

His work spans advertising, music videos, and narrative filmmaking. He has collaborated with international production companies such as Landia, Rebolución, and Primo on projects for both local and international clients.

His work in music videos has also been recognized with a nomination at BAMV FEST, highlighting his ability to create powerful visual identities across different formats.

He is particularly drawn to projects that explore the world and the intimate dimensions of human existence, approaching cinematography as a way of observing, shaping, and giving visual form to emotions.
